Overview

The administrator of Shimlar.org respects and works to protect your privacy!  The information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed.  The information you give will not be used in ways in which you have not consented.  A more detailed explanation is given below.  If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

The administrator of Shimlar.org does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or e-mail lists.

Information Automatically Logged:

Shimlar logs IP addresses for systems administration and security purposes. IP addresses are logged to track a user's session and behavior.  IP addresses are linked to player accounts which may include personally identifiable information including but not limited to e-mail addresses.  This is necessary to maintain the integrity of our server's security, blocking both the IP addresses and disabling player accounts of problem visitors.

Use of cookies:

For your convenience we use cookies on our site to store account names and optionally account passwords.  It is important to understand that this information is stored on your machine and thus can be retrieved by anyone who has access to your computer.  Your login name however is NEVER stored.   If for some reason you would rather not take advantage of the benefits made possible with cookies entirely, most browsers allow you to disable the use of cookies completely. Shimlar.org will however not work.
