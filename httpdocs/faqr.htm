Shimlar Reset Related Questions:

Q: What is reset?
A: A player wipe. All Characters, items and such are deleted. These only occur when a serious bug in the game is found and has been exploited by many people. We don't currently anticipate any further resets for this reason, though.

Q: Do I keep my items?
A: No.

Q: When?
A: Whenever it's needed, but as seldom as possible.

Q: So, I lose everything. All my xxx hours of playtime and no result?
A: It's a game... a waste of time anyways :)

Q: Do mods keep their powers? RP mods? Arches?
A: Mods and RP mods will have a choice to keep power or not. Arches will have a choice of mod or player.

Q: Will there be a warning before reset?
A: No, it's pointless since people will stop playing.

Q: Can I go and pk everyone now? Or shout bad words?
A: No. The game is still running, and a lot of features are not done. Bad behavior will get you muted or banned.
    Banned characters will not be able to reserve names.
