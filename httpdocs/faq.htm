1. General Questions.

Most Frequently Asked Question.

Q: How do i become a Mod?
A: Moderators are Chosen by Lord A and J.NicoletteCanne, after consulting other archwizards and some moderators. The choice is based on their understanding of the game and general skill in handling situations in chat. (Asking a Moderator, Arch Wizard or Admins WILL NOT make you a Mod, it will more than likely decrease your chance.)

Q: What Race do I choose?
A: Any race you like. Check the manual if you want to know the details.

Q. Where am i?
A. All players start in their races Starting Zone.

Q: How do I play?
A: You select a monster from a list, and click New Fight. Then you either click Attack or Cast.
     You get experience and gold from the creatures you kill.

Q: What happens if I die?
A: You lose all UNBANKED gold and 20% of exp needed to next level, however, exp can't go below 0.
     -certain accessories can decrease the % of lost exp or gold on death. (see God of War's help site accessories section)

Q: Why do I only hit kobolds for 10?
A: This game shows the REAL damage you dealt. This means that even if you did hit the monster for
     100000 damage, since it only lost 10 health, you see 10 damage.

Q: Why are there only 10 monsters? How do I get to other zones?
A: When you reach a certain level, you can go to Outworld from 0,0 in any zone.
     There are over 100 monsters for sure, but each zone only has a certain amount.
     You can also teleport to other zones if you are of high enough level and can afford it.

Q: Outworld, huh?
A: The game has a kind of world map, on which zones can be found. You can go to Outworld from 0,0 once
     you reach level 15. Check the Zone Map link for a E(Exit).

Q: Can I sell Spells?
A: Yes. It is done in weapon Shops.

Q: Can I have more than one character?
A:  Yes, as many as you like. However, low level characters are deleted from time to time.
     You are not allowed to actively level two characters at the same time. You can be online with more than one character but are only allowed to level one.

Q: I got item with [2]. How do I see what the gem is?
A: There is a function called "Examine" in the weapon Shop or under the Trade menu.

Q: Your mastery increased?
A: You got better at using the item.
Item Effect
   
Weapon Increases chance of Critical Hit, and increases accuracy (Dex, this doesn't effect the acutal Dex amount)
Spell Increases chance of Critical Hit, and increases accuracy (Wis, this doesn't effect the acutal Wis amount)
Armor every 5% armor mastery you gain +1 AC

Q: How many items I can carry?
A: 30 items max. Each item takes same space.

Q: What is a Veteran/ Fanatic/ etc. monster?
A: There is about 6% chance for a random special monster. Some have more gold, others
    have more stats, so learm what to kill and what to run from.

Q: What characters get deleted? How can I protect my mules?
A: Characters below level 100 AND below 10 million total gold (unbanked and banked) AND haven't logged in for 30 days
    are regularly cleaned up. So log in with the mule once a week or just give them over 10m gold and they
    are safe. Storing IX items and **** gems on mules is strongly discouraged and not recommended though, buy a house for
    those purposes, or get a character above level 100 to be sure.

Q. Now I'm dead...what next?
 A. Click on the Zone map link under your Equip list, and look for the square with an 'R' in it, then go there and click 'Revive'.

Q. I've got Gold in hand, I think I should bank it....How?
A. Click on the Zone map link under your Equip list, and look for the square with a 'B' in it, then go there and select 'Deposit All' and then click 'OK'.

Q. Deposit All?
A.
Deposit All Deposits All Gold in hand.
Deposit Deposits the amount you type in.
Withdraw Withdraws the amount you type in.
Withdraw All Withdraws All your Gold in Bank.

Q. Where is the Shop?
A. Click on the Zone map link under your Equip list, and look for the square with a 'S' then go and enjoy the rest of the day shopping.

Q. Where is the Magic Shop (Magic Tower)?
A: Click on the Zone map link under your Equip list, and look for the square with a 'M' then go and enjoy the rest of the day shopping.

Q. Where is the Exit, for the Zone I'm in?
A. Click on the Zone map link under your Equip list, and look for the square with a 'E' then go and enjoy your freedom.

Q. I've bought a Spell/Weapon, but I can't equip it, not enough NTL/STR or my Levels too low...why?
A. Exactly what it says, all items have Stat requirements, if you buy them and don't have the stats you can't equip.

Q. Okay, how do I find out there Stat/Level requirements?
A. Well there are 2 ways to find out (Asking in Chat isn't 1 of them, people will just tell you to do what's said below).

1. go to God of War's Help site and go to the equipment section.
2. when in the shop, select the item and click the examine button, then below it will display the items info.

Q. Okay I'm a Certain Race, what items are best for me?
A. All Race info can be found at God of War's Help site and click on RACES.

Q. Okay i just leveled what do i click on?
A. When you first start its advisable to raise Ntl or Str to equip better items as items have a Stat Requirement.

Weapons require STR
Spells require NTL
Armor requires VIT

Casters should Level: (over 10 levels)

· Wis x 5
· Ntl x 3
· Vit x 1

Fighters should Level: (over 10 levels)

· Dex x 5
· Str x 3
· Vit x 1

This is only a rough guide, different players may have other ways, it's your choice in the end.

Q. I'm Level 'XXX' what should I be fighting?
A. Don't ask this question, each player has his/her own limits, someone at level 100 may be able to kill a Druchii Guardsman in Ta'lorn where other may only be able to kill a Skulking Minion, its all down to Equip and Gem setup. Q. How do I heal?...and can I heal outside of battle?
A. Yes you can heal outside of battle, but only if you own a House on Estates hill. To heal in Battle you can either:

· equip a heal spell and use that.
· enchant your equip with a Ruby or Bloodrock gem  (Ruby is best for fast regeneration, but also costs more).
· upgrade Ghost Armor to Mystic Armor (Saintly Plate '++') ('++' = the level of the Ghost Armor).

4. Reincarnation.

Q: How do I Reincarnate?
A: Go to Royal University 14,21 Wilderness, enter and then go to 1,2 Royal University with 100k, then you can reincarnate.

Q: What's the drawback on Reincarnating?
A: You lose:

4 Levels
All Free Exp
20% off all Masteries (They wont drop below your races starting masteries). *

* If your race starts with 20% Armor and you have 30% when you reincarnate you'll have 20% after reincarnation.

Q: Why should i reincarnate?
A: The Reincarnation feature is for those that mess up there stats, or for those who wish to reincarnate as another Race with Level restrictions (For example: Balrog, Flame Demon, Genie) Check God of War's Help site for the level restrictions.


5. Fighting/Alignment Related Questions.

Q. How do I fight?
A. You can fight anywhere except the Bank, Shrine, Magic Shop and Weapon/Armor shop, to fight you simply click Fight Creature, select the enemy, then New Fight, from there you just click Attack/Cast.

Q. How do I duel?
A. You duel the same way as you Fight enemies, but instead of Fight Creature you select Duel Player.

Q. Who attacks first?
A.

Player Vs Monster: If you have more Dex/Wis than the enemy, then you will attack first...however if the enemy is a caster/fighter and your a fighter/caster then its 50/50 who will attack first.

Player Vs Player: Same as with the Monsters.

*note: the new gem (Jade) increases your initiative chance, initiative is the chance to hit/cast before enemy.

Q. What do I get from Dueling?
A. You get the Gold the person you're dueling has in hand, and they will lose 20% of their Free Exp, If you die you will loose the same.

Q. What happens to the Free Exp they loose, do I get it?
A. No you don't receive their lost Exp,

Q. I attacked someone again, but it says I can't kill anyone for 'XXX' amount of seconds....why?
A. After the reset a new feature was added to stop people PK'ing more than 6 people per hour, after you PK someone there is a 10min or 600seconds time limit until your next Kill.

Q. How do I know when I can kill again?
A. Attack someone again, if you can Attack/Cast against them your limit is up.

Q. Why are certain people Red/Blue in the Duel list?
A. Red people have - Alignment (Evil), PK'ing them will give you Good Alignment. Blue People have + Alignment (Good), PK'ing them will give you Evil Alignment.

Q. Can i get Good/Evil Alignment any other way
A. Yes, you get more Alignment from PK'ing people you receive +1 Alignment for killing evil (red) players and -1 Alignment for killing good (yellow) players, however fighting in certain zones give you + or - Alignment.

7. Quests.

Q. How do i get a quest?
A. You can get a quest by visiting the QM (Questmaster) in his hut at 20, 21 in the wilderness, once inside the quests available are listed at 1,2.

Q. I cant do another quest why not?
A. If you complete a Quest you have to wait 8hours before you can complete another quest 6hours if you have a certain clan bonus.

Q. I'm Muted, does this effect my Questing?
A. You have been punished for disobeying the rules when muted you cannot participate in a quest.

You cannot complete a Quest until the mute wears off.
If you have completed a Quest recently and your still waiting to complete another...the mute time is added to your time remaining.

Q. It says kill a certain enemy...what does it mean by Certain?
A. A Certain enemy means exactly that, its 1 of the special enemy types (i.e. Fool, Merchant, Enigma etc)

Q. Is there anyway of knowing what the certain enemy is?
A. Yes when you find the enemy and kill it. you get a bold white message in the battle screen saying you have comepleted a quest, go pick up your reward.

Q. What does it mean by level limit: XXX (XXX been the level)
A. If you are above that level you cannot do that quest, sorry :(

8. Gem related questions.

Q. I found a gem, yay me....what now?
A. You can equip gems, sell them or save them for a later stage in game.

Q. I found a Fighter/Caster Gem and i'm a Caster/Fighter, what should i do with it?
A. Best bet is to sell it to a Fighter/Caster or keep it as a trade for a Caster/Fighter gem you need.

Q. I found a * gem what's the * mean?
A. * = the gems grade, the higher the grade * - ** - *** - **** the most potent the gem.

for example,

FT* increases Armor Class (AC) by 1point.
FT**** increases Armor Class (AC) by 8points.

Q. How do i find * - **** gems
A. Certain gem grades can only be found in certain zones, **** can only be found in 2 zones, these are the best gems in the game and so are equally as har to get the 2 zones are extremely hard.

Gem Pond = Level 450 to enter Level 500 - 550+ is recommended (this all depends on your equip and gem setup)
Ancient Cemetary = Level 500 to enter 600 - 700 is recommended (this all depends on your equip and gem setup)

more info at God of War's Help site

Q. How do i use gems?
A. To enchant a gem to an item you go to the Magic Tower (Magic Shop) and un-equip the item you wish to place the gem into, select the gem then click enchant.

Q. I enchanted an item with the wrong gem (i deserve a medal for worlds biggest idiot) how do i get it out?
A. Firstly yes you are an idoit, but no medals are available, Secondly you can dis-enchant a gem from an item at 2 temples.

The temples are:

Temple of Shadows found at 15,21 in The Wilderness and needs level 80 to enter. (cannot enter if Alignment is +500)
Temple Of Order is found at 7,6 in Dimension of Light and needs level 350 to enter. (cannot enter if Alignment is -500)

Q. Do gems that steal stats add the stolen stats to my stat?
A. This is tricky, they do but only in battle, they DO NOT add the stat to your base stats, or physically change the value or your stats.

Q. Do Demon Fang's/Dragon Fangs still effect my Weapon/Spell if they are embedded in something other than my Weapon/Spell?
A. Yes the gems work no matter what item they are embedded into.
