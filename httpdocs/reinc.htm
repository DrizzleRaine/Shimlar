Shimlar Manual - Reincarnation

Reincarnation is a process of changing your race and stats. Perhaps you selected the wrong
stats, or just got bored of being a certain race. Maybe you wish to switch from mage to fighter or
the other way around. Or perhaps you maxxed your mastery and wish to develop it further by
selecting a race that can get it higher. Well, this is the way to do it.

Reincarnation is available to players with level of 100 or more. Certain races require a higher level, because
they can specialize in some skill to great extent. Every race gains the same amount of total stats a level,
some just gain more in certain stat. Every race gains the same amount of exp and gold a kill.

Reincarnation is availabe from the Royal University and costs 100,000 gold.
You can reincarnate as many times as you wish.

You get "free levels", the amount is your old level-5, and your free exp goes to 0 as well. So, basicaly you
lose 5 levels in the process.

Also, you lose 20% of every mastery, and your mastery can't be higher than your new race max. However, it
can't also go lower than the new race starting mastery, so you don't lose the masteries you don't use.
