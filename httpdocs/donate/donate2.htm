    • WHY
    Because you like the game... see it as a way of saying "Thank you". You're not forced to give anything. Really!! Hang around,
    have fun, play... whether you have money or not, there will be no difference. Only give money if you want to give... you will not
    get special powers (like mute) when you donate.

    WHO
    As a mechanism of control, and because of the time allocation, all donations will arrive at Lord ArPharazon's account.
    He will transfer the money to Toshax, and pay for the server.
    If you have special questions considering donations, mail Lord ArPharazon. Do not suggest deals please.
    We will not make a secret about the payments we receive. On the site will be a list showing the names and how much they
    donated. Since donations are totally free, and don't give the right to obtain any special treatment, this is a way of control.

    WHAT
    Donations are gifts... thus you're not able to ask for a refund. You must be aware of the fact your money will be used to make
    options available for all the players, rather than only for yourself.
    Being a gift, you will not get a direct return. You should not ask neither. We appreciate any help, but we're not selling out.
    Your money will be used the way that fits Toshax/Lord ArPharazon, but you can decide to which option your support goes.

    PayPal
    PayPal website
    PayPal is an American system of payments that doesn't reveal any Credit Card information. International accounts can be made
    too, but not in every country. Only credit cards are allowed in PayPal. The info shown in PayPal will not be made public,
    IF YOU ASK. If you don't mention it, you'll be listed amongst the other donators.
    Bank transfers should be possible from any European country. Better mail for more info.
