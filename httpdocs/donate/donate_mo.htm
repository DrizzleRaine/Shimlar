Money orders



This is a system provided by www.sendmoneyorder.com.
I'll be honest, in Europe we're not familiar with Money Orders (my bank asked what it was), and I found this site that seems to make it easy.

As far as I understood it, they make it possible to pay them with a bank account, a CC or even a PayPal account. Then they sent me a money order. Just to make things clear... PayPal is preferred, but as these people allow to pay with checks and bank accounts, I guess some of you might use it.


How it works
  • Fill in your character name, and the thing you want to donate for (like A (for server), B (for multigem), ...)
    So, if I wanted to donate for A - server, I would write: "Lord ArPharazon - A"
  • Fill in the amount you want to donate
  • Click on "Send payment"
  • You'll have to make a new account... sorry... but it's a one page operation
  • Then you can add a bankaccount in your Memberschip ID and donate!! yay!!
