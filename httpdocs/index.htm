Hi,

for some of you, this is the second time they see such a white page instead of the usual black one. For others, it's the first time. Don't feel bad about it :) The message is the same.
You can continue reading, or click here to fast forward to the game.
Just FYI: the policies inside the game don't change. We can still mute and/or ban.
You might have noticed I didn't answer emails anymore. I stopped doing so on 22/06/2007. You probably wonder why? Well. I don't know. I just felt like... this is it. It's the end. I signed my contract as lawyer, and I knew I needed to free more time for work. Stopping with the email was one step.
Other reasons influenced my decision as well.

  • I kept banning cheaters.
  • Financial support... I coded some new things, nothing changed. Ok, no one wants to pay anymore.
  • Some people think they control it all... well, it's not true as you can see.
  • The game is now 6 years old. It would need a major overhaul, as technology largely passed us.

What will happen now? Mmmmm... I remember how I felt when RaceWarKingdoms, the original, true and free version, closed. Clerity wrote a beautiful poem about it. It was a very very very sad day... but also the start of Shimlar. Since then, many other people started creating games. Toshax and I influenced a lot of people. And so did the many others who participated here. All the archwizards, all the moderators, all the players, and, last but not least, those who passed away.
I do hope that playing in a community like Shimlar formed parts of you in a good way. We at least tried to keep it a friendly place to play.

To return to my point. RWK literally was removed from the server, entirely, without warning whatsoever. I now warn thee.
The contract with the provider ends on 08/11/2007. That's the eight of november. The server will be shut down at 6 P.M. CST (GMT -6). You can convert it to your timezone by clicking here.

What will happen? I'll keep a copy of the code and the database for myself. Who knows what might happen in the future. Please do not ask me for (pieces of) the code. It's not open source. It never was. It's not meant to be shared.

Once again, I would like to sincerely thank all those who contributed to the game's existence the past six years. Please do not donate anymore, it won't change my mind. Also, many thanks to all those who were my eyes, ears and opinion in the game the last six years. I can't be online all the time, it never would have run until now without the mods and archwizards.

Thank you all for being part of my life. I'm 30 since 25/10/2007, and I have to move on with my personal life as I did professionally. I hope you'll find another new home as I did when RWK shut down. I wish ye all the very very best in life.

Lord Ar-Pharazôn
Shimlar admin.
